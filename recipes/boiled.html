Boiled eggs recipe

Description

This is about cooking eggs into an easy and nourishing meal but this time it's just boiled

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • water
  • a pan
  • salt
  • spices
  • vegetables like onion, garlic or tomatoes

Steps

  1. Put the water and the eggs together in a pot
  2. Let it boil for 15 minutes
  3. Serve and Enjoy
