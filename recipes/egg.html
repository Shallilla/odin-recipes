Omellette recipe

Description

This is about cooking eggs into an easy and nourishing meal called omelette.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • oil or butter
  • a pan
  • salt
  • spices
  • vegetables like onion, garlic or tomatoes

Steps

  1. First break the eggs and pour the content in a bowl
  2. add the salt, the spices and the neatly cutteed veggies
  3. MIx well till a homogenous result
  4. Heat a pan with some oil or butter
  5. pour a moderate quantity in the pan just enough to cover everything and let it cook until it becomes solid
  6. turn the other side of the omelette so that it can cook too
  7. Repeat the 5th and 6th steps
  8. Serve and Enjoy
